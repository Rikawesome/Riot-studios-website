WHAT IS RIOT STUDIOS ?

Riot studios is an app development company that was created by Four students in the year 2023.It was created in thier bid to apply their skills to help solving problems through apps and software, heads of this motion and the founders of Riot Studios are Ronald Kanu And Chibuike George who founded the Company while is thier First year in high School(ie. 10th grade or SS1) while thinking about a game idea that came to mind, the rest of the team members joined later on as the company evolved. It currently has no completed project but has an upcoming app which you can find out more about in the HOME page. Riot Studios currently has no Headquaters at the moment but looks forward to having one in the future.
